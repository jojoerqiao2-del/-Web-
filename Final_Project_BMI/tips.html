Health Tips & Advice

Based on generic BMI categories, here are some recommendations.

If you are Underweight (BMI < 18.5)

Aim to gain weight gradually by eating healthy, energy-dense foods. Include more protein, nuts, and dairy in your diet to build muscle mass rather than just fat.

Watch Related Video

If you are Normal Weight (18.5 - 23)

Great job! Maintain your weight by staying active for at least 30 minutes a day. Focus on a balanced diet rich in vegetables, fruits, and whole grains.

Watch Related Video

If you are Overweight (BMI > 23)

Try to reduce sugar intake and control portion sizes. Combining cardio exercises (like running or swimming) with a healthy diet is the best way to lose weight sustainably.
